Our History

The Founding of Kenneth Motors

Established in 2018, Kenneth Motors began with a singular vision: to curate the world's finest automobiles for Nigeria's most discerning drivers.

What started as a private concierge service has evolved into a premier dealership, bridging the gap between international luxury markets and local automotive excellence. We don't just sell cars; we deliver a legacy of performance.
