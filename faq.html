Frequently Asked Questions

General Inquiries

+ How does the online purchase process work?

Our online system allows you to browse inventory, calculate financing, and proceed to a simulated checkout. Once you "Place Final Order," a sales representative will contact you within one business hour to finalize the paperwork, secure payment, and arrange delivery of your vehicle.

+ Are the vehicle prices final?

The prices listed are the base sale prices. They do not include sales tax (calculated at checkout based on a configurable rate), registration fees, or a standard $995 processing fee. These amounts are detailed in the final summary on the checkout page.

Financing & Delivery

+ What happens after I request a Test Drive?

Once you submit the Test Drive form on the product detail page, your preferred date and contact information are sent directly to our VIP concierge team. A representative will call or email you promptly to confirm the date, time, and location for your private test drive.

+ Can I get the vehicle delivered to my home?

Yes. Kenneth Motors provides nationwide white-glove delivery service. The cost of delivery is generally included in the final price, depending on your location. Your sales representative will detail all shipping costs prior to finalizing your order.

+ Is the financing calculator accurate?

The calculator provides an **estimate** based on standard annuity formulas and assumed inputs (interest rate and term). Your actual monthly payment will depend on your final approved loan amount, credit score, and current market rates.
